6. Cookies

DSANotes uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to understand how our site is used and to improve your experience.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site to function; cannot be disabled
  • Analytics — help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics); these are anonymized

You can control or disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

7. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with DSANotes. We have enabled IP anonymization, meaning your full IP address is never stored. Data collected is used only to improve our content and site experience.

Google Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). You may opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
